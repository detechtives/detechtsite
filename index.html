What we do

How do we help small businesses be more successful?

Accurate Forecasting

Business owners shouldn't have to be data wizards to determine the future of their business. deTECHt provides accurate forecasting to help owners have exceptional clarity into their business operations.

Actionable Insights

Graphs and charts can be tough to interpret. deTECHt provides clear, actionable insights to help remove the stress of making minute decisions from store owners.

Offline A/B Testing

Selecting products in stores can be a lot of guesswork. deTECHt provides the ability to determine in a systematic manner which products provide the greatest benefit to your store.
